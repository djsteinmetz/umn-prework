Prince Rogers Nelson Fan Page

Prince Rogers Nelson was an American singer, songwriter, musician, record producer, actor, and director. He was born June 7th 1958 in Minneapolis, Minnesota. He died on April 21st 2016 in Chanhassen, Minnesota at the age of 57. He was beloved in the local community as well as internationally recognized as one of the most genius minds in the industry.

Prince dedicated his life to music and performing. Prince released 39 studio albums, 4 live albums, 17 vidoe albums, and 1 remix album. Throughout his career, Prince accumulated five U.S. number-one singles and 15 worldwide #1 hits, as well as 8 worldwide number one albums.

(Sources: Prince (Musician) Wikipedia, Prince Discography Wikipedia)
